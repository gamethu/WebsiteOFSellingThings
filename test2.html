Product Description

Indulge in the golf course's lush greens with the Jordan 1 Retro Low Golf Travis Scott Neutral Olive, a stellar collaboration between the iconic Jordan brand and the renowned rapper, Travis Scott. This is the first-ever golf shoe collaboration between Jordan and Travis Scott, making it a rare treasure in the sneaker community.

Reflecting Cactus Jack’s signature earth-toned color palette, this sneaker captivates the crowd with its olive green shade harmoniously blended with sail-tinted leather overlays, a contrasting black reverse Swoosh logo, and subtle touches of bright volt green on the tongue label and heel logo. Crafted for golf enthusiasts, yet with a design that resonates with every sneakerhead, this pair is tailored for the greens with altered traction and hardened rubber. However, the cool and casual vibe it carries makes it suitable for off-the-green casual struts, giving Travis Scott fans a chance to sport these beauties anytime, anywhere.

Dropped on October 13, 2023, at a retail price of $170, the Jordan 1 Retro Low Golf Travis Scott Neutral Olive is not just a pair of sneakers; it's a statement of style, comfort, and unrivaled collaboration between sportswear and music royalty.
